Work differently

Mangrove was founded on common values: Freedom, Goodwill and Transparency. These values are the roots of our community. They feed our culture, our projects and our agile structure.
Unlike most companies, we collaborate remotely and without hierarchy. Such decentralization is enabled by new processes & technology: we build our own tools to maximize transparency and task-automatization across the ecosystem.
